Welcome to our company

Corporate Mission


Our mission is "Connecting the World, Creating Prosperity Together." Through import and export trade, we not only facilitate the circulation of goods globally but also contribute to the economic development of different countries and regions. We are dedicated to being a bridge in international trade, helping our clients expand their markets and achieve their business goals.

Corporate Vision


Our vision is to become a "Leader in the Field of International Trade." We will continue to innovate and optimize our services, leading industry development by continuously learning and adapting to market changes. We believe that through relentless effort and teamwork, Quark Industrial will hold an important position on the global trade stage.

About Us

Company Overview


Established in 2024, Ningbo Quark Technology Co., Ltd is located in Ningbo City, an important port city on the eastern coast of China. As a dynamic industrial company, we specialize in import and export trade and are committed to providing our clients with efficient and professional services and solutions.We have branches located in Hong Kong, Vietnam, and North America.
